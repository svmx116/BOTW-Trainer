A trainer for The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild on Wii U using TCP Gecko.

---

## Requirements

- Wii U with Tiramisu
- TCP Gecko
- Breath of the Wild (Wii U)

---

## Installation

1. Install TCP Gecko on your SD card
2. Launch TCP Gecko from Homebrew Launcher and press **X**
3. Launch Breath of the Wild
4. Open the trainer on your PC
5. Enter your Wii U IP and click **Connect**

---

## Features

Edit health, stamina, and rupees

Modify weapons and inventory

Moon Jump, speed

Infinite resources

**And more!**
